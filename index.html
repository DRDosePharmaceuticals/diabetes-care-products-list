Diabetes Care Products

Our Diabetes Care portfolio offers trusted, effective treatments designed for managing Type 2 Diabetes with a focus on quality, safety, and patient wellbeing. Explore our comprehensive range of pharmaceutical products, crafted to support blood sugar control and improve patient outcomes.

Glimidose-M1

Composition: Glimepiride (1mg) + Metformin Hydrochloride SR (500mg)

Description: Used to treat Type-2 Diabetes mellitus in adults. Helps control blood sugar levels effectively. Combination of sulfonylurea and biguanide. Supports insulin production and reduces liver glucose output.

Usage Instructions: Take as directed by your physician. Swallow with water after meals. Do not crush or chew the tablet.

Storage & Safety: Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep out of reach of children. Use only under medical supervision.

Precautions: Not recommended for Type 1 diabetes. Inform your doctor about any kidney or liver issues. Monitor blood sugar levels regularly.

Vildose 50 MG

Composition: Vildagliptin (50mg)

Description: Vildose 50 is a DPP-4 inhibitor used to manage type 2 diabetes mellitus. It helps improve glycemic control by increasing levels of incretin hormones, which stimulate insulin release and suppress glucagon secretion.

Usage Instructions: Take orally with or without food as prescribed by physician. Follow dosage schedule strictly to maintain blood sugar levels.

Storage & Safety: Store below 30°C in a dry place. Keep away from children and pets. Avoid exposure to sunlight and moisture.

Precautions: Not recommended for Type 1 diabetes or for treating diabetic ketoacidosis. Inform your doctor if you have kidney or liver issues. Regularly monitor blood sugar levels during treatment. Use with caution during pregnancy or breastfeeding - only under medical supervision.

Marketed by: DR Dose Pharmaceuticals
